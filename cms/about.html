Why we are, what we are

About us

DDSR Builders & Developers LLP is redefining the skyline of Navi Mumbai with a portfolio of quality projects that epitomize affordable luxury and convenience. With five years of industry experience, we have cultivated a deep understanding of our customers' aspirations, delivering homes that perfectly balance style, preference, and budget.

Our expertise extends beyond completed residences to residential plots, where we leverage our profound knowledge to identify strategically located lands abundant in natural resources and poised for high appreciation.

DDSR Builders & Developers LLP is dedicated to expanding its footprint across Kalamboli, Kamothe, Kharghar, New Panvel, and beyond into the Thane-Kalyan belt. Our team shares a singular ambition: to make superior living affordable and accessible to the masses. We believe in making dreams come true, offering well-planned, free-flowing living spaces that enhance everyday life. Our commitment to quality and meticulous planning ensures we deliver homes that bring joy and satisfaction to our customers.

Join us at DDSR Builders & Developers LLP, where we strive to create beautiful spaces and see more smiles.

Our Mission

  • To create a lifestyle infrastructure that’s environment, social, wellness and convenience-friendly.
  • To locate our projects within striking distance of connectivity guaranteeing them as good investments for the future.
  • To lay special emphasis on detailing, ensuring our customers receive every sq. yard and sq. feet they pay for while purchasing a plot or flat respectively.
  • To ensure for them a property that comes with value additions in terms of either world-class amenities or branded fixtures and fittings OR both.

Our Ambitions.

At DDSR Builders & Developers LLP, we envision becoming the benchmark for design and architectural excellence in residential, commercial, and retail infrastructure across Navi Mumbai. Join us as we shape the future of this vibrant city.
