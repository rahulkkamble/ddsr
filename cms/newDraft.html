Digital Communications Collaborative Consortium (D3C) :

A Call to Innovate and Collaborate

For over 28 years, COAI has been at the forefront of the Indian telecom and broadband sector, steering its core engine from regulatory and policy perspectives. Our mission has always been to ensure seamless collaboration between the government, industry and various other stakeholders, all working together towards the monumental task of nation-building. The Association remains steadfast in its dedication to advancing modern communications by establishing world-class mobile infrastructure, products and services, thereby delivering the benefits of innovative and affordable mobile communications to the people of India.

In this spirit of progress, COAI is proud to announce a new initiative aimed at bringing together a diverse array of stakeholders, including Academia/Research Institutes and R&D, start-ups and MSMEs. The primary goal of this forum is to create a unified platform where committed stakeholders can collaborate with COAI to explore opportunities, drive innovation, and tackle operational challenges at various levels. This initiative will foster a better understanding and knowledge base through collaborative efforts.
