FAQ

1. Is it mandatory to sign an agreement and get empanelled to start doing business with DDSR ?

It is mandatory to sign the Channel Partner Agreement and get empanelled before you can start promoting and selling DDSR properties.

2. Can empanelment be done without RERA ?

RERA Certificate is mandatory to get empaneled with DDSR Group for promoting DDSR properties.

3. What are mandatory documents for Channel Partner empanelment?

Approved original RERA certificate, PAN Card (As per the entity type), Address proof of the person or the organisation (as per RERA name) GST certificate or No-GST Declaration (whichever is applicable) and Cancelled cheque.
